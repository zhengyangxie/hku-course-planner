    How it works

    1
    Pick your programs
    Drag any program from "Available Programs" into "My Degree". You can combine up to 288 credits (e.g., Math Major + CS Major + Physics Minor).
    2
    Plan your semesters
    Search or filter courses in the Course Pool, then drag them into the 8 semester slots. On mobile, tap a course and choose a semester.
    3
    Validate
    Watch the dashboard for credit progress. Red cards show overloads; warnings list missing prerequisites. Click "Check Graduation" for the full requirement audit.
    4
    Save & share
    Your plan auto-saves to this browser. Use "Export TXT" to share with friends or advisors.
